### 3. ğŸ¥‰ Ikigai Framework (Ikigai-9 + Purpose Discovery Quizzes)

**The Framework:**
The Japanese concept of ikigai ("a reason for being") maps the intersection of four circles:
- **What you LOVE** (Passion)
- **What you're GOOD AT** (Vocation/Talent)
- **What the WORLD NEEDS** (Mission)
- **What you can be PAID FOR** (Profession)

Where all four overlap = your Ikigai. The intersections also produce:
- Passion + Talent = Satisfaction but no wealth
- Talent + Payment = Comfortable but unfulfilled
- Payment + Mission = Excitement but uncertainty
- Mission + Passion = Delight but insecurity

**The Assessments:**
1. **Ikigai-9 Scale** â€” FREE, validated, 9-item scored questionnaire
   - Academically validated by Fido, Kotera, & Asano (2019)
   - Measures three dimensions: Optimistic emotions toward life, Active attitudes toward future, Meaning of existence
   - 9 statements scored on a Likert scale
   - Simple but psychometrically robust

2. **Ikigai Test (ikigaitest.com)** â€” FREE, online, visual results
   - URL: https://ikigaitest.com/
   - Produces a personal Ikigai diagram with Mission, Passion, Profession, Vocation
   - Includes personality type and career suggestions

3. **IDRLabs Ikigai Test** â€” FREE, visual
   - URL: https://www.idrlabs.com/ikigai/test.php
   - Structured reflection on passion, talent, values, livelihood
